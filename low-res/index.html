pixelated visualizer for 3D models.
inspired by retro games and early painting programs.
built with three.js and glsl.

directions:
Rhino 3dm or generic glb/gltf files only. Solid mesh geometry only (no curves).
for best results center model at world origin (0, 0, 0).
pan + zoom with mouse or touch.
use control panel to customize the display.
